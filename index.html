Purpose

CityImpact is a mobile application that enables users to host events to Impact their own communities. Functionality, ease-of-use, and an intuitive user interface will make it easy for users to host events and join events that are going on in their own communities.

  • Check us out on Github

Make an Impact

This app was developed in order to promote community service and collaboration in the community to make a positive impact on the world.

  • Connect

    Meet new people in your community through locally held events.

  • Impact

    Impact your community by hosting or joining events.

  • Awareness

    Keep an eye on the pulse of your community.

Create events and get the whole community involved!

We have all the tools you need to plan and organize your next community service event including an interactive map with markers for events linking to host and event details. Utilizing the AirBnB maps API, we give you the ability to host your own community service event in your neighborhood or whereever you can make the most impact!
